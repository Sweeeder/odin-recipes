The Dog Bowl

Description

This is a lovely dog bowl by DaddyWellness

Here are the ingredients

Ingredients

  • 500g of ground beef
  • Salt and hot curry spice
  • Parmegiano Reggiano
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Add the salt, hot curry spice, and Parmegiano to the mixture
  3. Top it up with two eggs sunny side up
